Blender Hollandaise Sauce

Description

Paragraph or two

about blender hollandaise sauce

Ingredients

  • Egg Yolks
  • Lemon Juice
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Hot Sauce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Blend the first four ingredients.
  2. Melt the butter in the microwave.
  3. Turn on the blender and pour in the butter.
